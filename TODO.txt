==== Conventions ====

Quotes
``...''      non-vocab terms, actual quotations

Bold
{\bf ...}    vocab terms, list items   replace with \textbf?

Fixed width
\java{...}   code (inline)

Italic
{\it ...}    filenames, titles         replace with \textit?
{\em ...}    emphasis, hints           replace with \emph?

Serif
{\sf ...}    GUI, keystrokes           replace with \textsf?
